.. zephyr:board:: esp32c3_lckfb

Overview
********

LCKFB ESP32-C3 is a development board based on ESP32-C3-WROOM-02 module,
which integrates 4 MB SPI flash. This board integrates complete Wi-Fi and
Bluetooth Low Energy functions.
