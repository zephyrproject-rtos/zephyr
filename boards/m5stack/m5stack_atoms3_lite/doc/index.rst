.. zephyr:board:: m5stack_atoms3_lite

Overview
********

M5Stack AtomS3 Lite is an ESP32-based development board from M5Stack.

Hardware
********

It features the following integrated components:

- ESP32-S3FN8 chip (240MHz dual core, Wi-Fi/BLE 5.0)
- 512KB of SRAM
- 384KB of ROM
- 8MB of Flash
- RGB Status-LED
