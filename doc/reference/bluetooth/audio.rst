.. _bluetooth_audio:

Bluetooth LE Audio
##################

The Bluetooth LE Audio :kconfig:`CONFIG_BT_AUDIO` adds audio support for
Bluetooth Low Energy bearer, which includes both stream and content control.
This modules implements the
`Bluetooth LE Audio Specifications <https://www.bluetooth.com/specifications/le-audio/>`_.

Read more about Bluetooth LE Audio on the
`Bluetooth SIG Website <https://www.bluetooth.com/bluetooth-resources/?tags=le-audio>`_.
